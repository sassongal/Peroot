## Overall Impression

Public face is confident and distinctly its own (obsidian/gold instrument). Biggest opportunity: internal consistency — admin + a few marketing accents predate the design system and reach for purple-gradient/gray defaults the brand rejects. The two most-used components (result section, mode selector) bounce, fighting the "precision instrument" character.

## What's Working
1. Hebrew-first RTL genuinely excellent (H2=4).
2. Engine-mode selector is a real signature (one hue at a time).
3. Public pages hold the line (4 detector hits total).
